Mathematical Foundation

Topics Covered:

  • Unit - 1 : Differential Calculus: Successive Differentiation, Leibnitz Theorem, Expansion of function of one variable in Taylor's and McLaurin's infinite series, Maxima and minima of functions of one variable, Partial Derivatives, Euler's theorem, change of variables, Total differentiation, Taylor's series in two variables, Maxima and Minima of two variables.
  • Unit - 2 : Integral Calculus: Integration by substitution, Integration by parts, Definite integrals, Reduction formulae, Double integrals, Triple integrals, Change of order of integration.
  • Unit - 3 : Vector Calculus: Scalar and vector fields, Gradient, Divergence and Curl, Line integrals, Surface integrals, Volume integrals, Green's theorem in a plane, Gauss divergence theorem, Stoke's theorem.
  • Unit - 4 : Ordinary Differential Equations: Formation of differential equations, Order and degree of differential equations, Linear differential equations, Homogeneous linear differential equations, Non-homogeneous linear differential equations, Applications of ordinary differential equations.
